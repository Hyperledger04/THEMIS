# GOLD-STANDARD TEMPLATE: General Legal Notice / Demand Notice

## Format Rules for This Document
- DO NOT number the header fields (From, To, Date, Subject). They are letterhead elements.
- Number only the substantive body paragraphs (1, 2, 3 ... onwards).
- Use formal, assertive advocate prose — not bullet points, not checklists.
- Every monetary amount must appear in both figures and words: ₹5,00,000 (Rupees Five Lakh only).
- Time periods must appear in both digits and words: 30 (thirty) days.
- The advocate's enrollment number is mandatory.

---

## TEMPLATE

[ADVOCATE LETTERHEAD]
[Advocate's full name, Enrollment No., Chamber address, phone, email]

Date: [DD Month YYYY]

LEGAL NOTICE

From:
[Client's full name], [S/o or D/o], [age], [full address]
Through their Advocate: [Advocate's full name], Enrollment No. [___], [chamber address]

To:
[Recipient's full name]
[Designation, if applicable]
[Full address]

Sub: Legal Notice for [brief one-line subject — e.g., Recovery of ₹[amount] / Breach of Agreement dated [date] / Vacation of Premises].

Sir/Madam,

Under the instructions and on behalf of my client, [CLIENT NAME], I hereby serve upon you this Legal Notice as under:

1. [Identity of client and relationship with the noticee. Who is the client, who is the noticee, and how are they related — commercially, contractually, or personally.]

2. [Background facts — concise chronological narrative. What transaction or relationship gave rise to the present dispute. Include dates, amounts, agreements, or communications relevant to establishing the cause of action.]

3. [The specific default, wrong, or breach. What exactly did the noticee do or fail to do, and when. Be precise — state dates, amounts, and the specific obligation breached.]

4. [Applicable legal basis. Identify the contractual clause, statutory provision, or legal principle the noticee has violated. E.g., "Clause [__] of the Agreement dated [date]" or "Section [__] of [Act], [Year]".]

5. [Prior attempts at resolution, if any. Whether my client called upon, wrote to, or otherwise approached the noticee to resolve the matter, and the noticee's response or failure to respond.]

6. I am therefore instructed by my client to and hereby call upon you to [STATE THE SPECIFIC DEMAND — pay ₹[amount] / vacate the premises at [address] / perform [specific obligation] / desist from [specific act]] within [___] ([in words]) days from the date of receipt of this notice.

7. Take further notice that if you fail to comply with the aforesaid demand within the period stipulated above, my client will be constrained to initiate appropriate legal proceedings against you before the competent court(s)/forum(s) — civil and/or criminal as may be advised — entirely at your risk, cost and consequences, without any further notice to you. My client expressly reserves all rights and remedies available under law.

This notice is being dispatched to you by Registered Post Acknowledgement Due and Speed Post. A copy is retained in my client's file.

Yours faithfully,

Sd/-
[ADVOCATE'S FULL NAME]
Enrollment No.: [___]
[Chamber address]
[Date]
