# GOLD-STANDARD TEMPLATE: Bail Application under Cr.P.C.

## Format Rules
- Use numbered paragraphs for all substantive content.
- Bold the prayer.
- Include verification.
- For S.437: regular bail (non-bailable offence, sessions/magistrate court)
- For S.438: anticipatory bail (Sessions Court / High Court)

---

## TEMPLATE

IN THE COURT OF THE [SESSIONS JUDGE / ADDITIONAL SESSIONS JUDGE / JUDICIAL MAGISTRATE FIRST CLASS], [CITY/DISTRICT]

[Case No. / FIR No.]: [___] of [Year]

IN THE MATTER OF:

[Applicant's full name], S/o [Father's name], aged [__] years, R/o [full address] ... APPLICANT/ACCUSED

Versus

State of [State name] ... RESPONDENT/STATE

APPLICATION UNDER SECTION [437/438] OF THE CODE OF CRIMINAL PROCEDURE, 1973 FOR [REGULAR/ANTICIPATORY] BAIL

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

1. That the applicant/accused is named in FIR No. [___] dated [date], registered at Police Station [name], [district], under Sections [___] of [Act/IPC/BNS], on the complaint of [complainant's name].

2. That the applicant has been [arrested and is in judicial custody since [date] at [prison name]] / [apprehending arrest in connection with the aforesaid FIR and filing this application before arrest under Section 438 Cr.P.C.].

3. That the applicant denies having committed any offence as alleged. The FIR is [false / motivated / filed to settle a personal score / filed under pressure of the complainant for extraneous reasons].

4. That the brief facts of the case, as disclosed in the FIR, are as follows: [Factual summary of what the FIR alleges, kept neutral and brief.]

5. That the applicant has deep roots in the community. The applicant is [describe ties — family, employment, property ownership in the area]. The applicant has no prior criminal antecedents / [state any prior acquittals or pending matters if relevant].

6. That the applicant is not likely to flee justice or tamper with evidence. [State reasons — fixed abode, family obligations, employment, readiness to surrender passport or report to the police periodically as conditions of bail.]

7. That the offence alleged is [bailable/non-bailable]. The maximum sentence prescribed is [___]. [For S.437: State whether the accused is woman, sick, infirm, or under 16 years of age — as these are special considerations under S.437(1) proviso.] [For S.438: The accusation of [offence] would expose the applicant to imprisonment and humiliation without adequate basis.]

8. That the investigation is [complete / substantially complete / at a nascent stage], and the applicant's custody is no longer required for investigative purposes. [If chargesheet filed: The chargesheet has been filed on [date] and trial has commenced/is yet to commence.]

9. That the applicant undertakes to:
   (a) Abide by all conditions imposed by this Hon'ble Court;
   (b) Not tamper with evidence or influence witnesses;
   (c) Appear before the Investigating Officer/Court as and when directed;
   (d) Not leave [city/state/country] without prior permission of this Hon'ble Court.

10. That the applicant has not filed any previous bail application before any court, OR [that a previous bail application was filed as [Case No. ___] before the [court] and was [rejected on [date] on the grounds of [___]], and the present application is filed on the changed circumstances that [___].]

(a) **Grant [regular/anticipatory] bail** to the applicant in connection with FIR No. [___] dated [date], P.S. [name], under Sections [___], on such terms and conditions as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper; and

(b) Pass such other and further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper in the facts and circumstances of the case.
