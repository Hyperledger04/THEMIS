# Litigation Pleading Style — Indian Courts Standard

## Page Setup
- Size: A4 (210mm × 297mm)
- Margins: Top 25mm, Bottom 25mm, Left 38mm (for court binding), Right 25mm
- Orientation: Portrait

## Typography
- Body/averments: Times New Roman 12pt, justified
- Cause title (parties): Times New Roman 12pt Bold, centred
- Court heading: Times New Roman 14pt Bold, ALL CAPS, centred
- Section headings (PETITION, FACTS, GROUNDS): Times New Roman 12pt Bold,
  ALL CAPS
- Prayer: Times New Roman 12pt, justified; "PRAYER" label in Bold

## Spacing
- Averments: 1.5 line spacing, 6pt space-after (Delhi HC standard)
- Double spacing between distinct sections (Facts, Grounds, Prayer)

## Standard Cause Title Format
```
IN THE HON'BLE [COURT NAME]
AT [CITY]

[MATTER TYPE] NO. ____ OF [YEAR]

IN THE MATTER OF:

[PETITIONER/PLAINTIFF FULL NAME]                    …PETITIONER/PLAINTIFF

                        VERSUS

[RESPONDENT/DEFENDANT FULL NAME]                    …RESPONDENT/DEFENDANT
```

## Pleading Structure
1. Court heading
2. Cause title
3. Opening line: "MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:" (writ / civil) or
   "RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:" (criminal)
4. Numbered averments: 1, 2, 3… (Arabic numerals, no sub-numbering at top
   level; use (a), (b) for sub-points within an averment)
5. Grounds (separately headed): A, B, C… (capitals)
6. Prayer
7. Verification
8. Affidavit (if required)

## Averment Numbering
- Top-level: 1. 2. 3.
- Sub-points within averment: (a) (b) (c)
- No Arabic sub-numbering (1.1, 1.2) in pleadings — court convention is
  alpha sub-points

## Prayer Format
```
PRAYER

It is, therefore, most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may
be pleased to:

(a) [Relief 1];

(b) [Relief 2];

(c) Pass any other order(s) as this Hon'ble Court may deem fit and proper
    in the facts and circumstances of the case.
```

## Verification
```
VERIFICATION

Verified at [CITY] on this [DATE] that the contents of paragraphs 1 to
[N] are true to my knowledge and belief, and the contents of paragraphs
[N+1] onwards are believed to be true on the basis of information
received and believed to be correct.

                                        [PETITIONER/PLAINTIFF]
                                        DEPONENT
```

## Page Numbers
Footer, right-aligned: "[X]"  (page number only, no "of N" in pleadings)
Font: Times New Roman 10pt
