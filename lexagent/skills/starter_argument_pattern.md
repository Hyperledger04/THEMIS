# Starter Argument Pattern Skill

## Description
Standard patterns for legal argumentation to ensure clarity and logic, adaptable for any matter type.

## Example Instructions
- Begin with a concise statement of the relief sought and legal grounds.
- State facts chronologically and relate to each legal element.
- For every legal issue, provide a pointwise argument: (1) legal principle, (2) relevant precedent or statute, (3) clear application to the facts, (4) risk/adverse case acknowledgment.
- End with a clear, actionable prayer.

# Example Usage
"In recovery suits, start by establishing admission of liability, then quantify claim, cite leading cases, address potential defenses, end with draft prayer."
