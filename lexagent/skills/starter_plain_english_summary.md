# Starter Plain English Summary Skill

## Description
How to summarize complex legal reasoning or risks in clear, simple English for non-lawyers or clients. Linked to risk highlighting as well.

## Example Instructions
- Summarize the legal impact and practical next steps in two paragraphs or less.
- Avoid legal jargon. Use everyday language.
- Identify any potential adverse outcomes or risks, clearly outlining them.

# Example Usage
"Explain why the relief is likely or unlikely to be granted, in plain language a client can understand."
