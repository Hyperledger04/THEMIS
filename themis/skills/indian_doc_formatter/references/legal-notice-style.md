# Legal Notice Style — Indian Standard

## Page Setup
- Size: A4
- Margins: Top 25mm, Bottom 25mm, Left 31.75mm, Right 25mm
- Orientation: Portrait

## Typography
- Body: Times New Roman 12pt, justified
- Heading (LEGAL NOTICE): Times New Roman 14pt Bold, ALL CAPS, centred
- Subject line: Times New Roman 12pt Bold
- "Without Prejudice" marker: Times New Roman 11pt Italic, top-right

## Standard Structure
```
[FIRM LETTERHEAD / ADVOCATE NAME + ENROLMENT NO.]
[ADDRESS]
[PHONE / EMAIL]

                                        Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

WITHOUT PREJUDICE

To,
[NOTICEE NAME]
[ADDRESS]

Through: [Registered Post / Speed Post / Email]

Re: [Brief subject — one line]

LEGAL NOTICE

Dear Sir/Madam,

Under instructions from and on behalf of my client, [CLIENT NAME], I
hereby issue you this Legal Notice as under:

1.  [Background facts]

2.  [Specific grievance / breach]

3.  [Demand / remedy sought]

You are hereby called upon to [specific action] within [15/30] days from
the date of receipt of this notice, failing which my client shall be
constrained to initiate appropriate legal proceedings against you before
the competent court/forum, at your risk, cost, and consequences.

This notice is issued without prejudice to all other rights and remedies
available to my client under law.

                                        Yours faithfully,

                                        [ADVOCATE NAME]
                                        Advocate
                                        Enrolment No.: [NUMBER]
                                        For and on behalf of [CLIENT NAME]
```

## Statutory Period Notes
- S.138 NI Act (cheque bounce): 15 days demand notice — do NOT change to
  30 days unless client explicitly instructs (30 days is not the statutory
  period)
- Consumer disputes: vary by forum; confirm with matter context
- Service of notice: always specify mode (RPAD / Speed Post / email)

## Spacing
- Body paragraphs: 1.15 line spacing, 6pt space-after
- Before numbered paragraphs: consistent — do not mix indented and
  non-indented paragraphs in the same notice

## Page Numbers
Footer, centred: "Page [X] of [Y]"
